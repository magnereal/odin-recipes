Bolognese

Description

This is an italian dish, made of pasta and meat stew

Ingredients

  • Pasta 200 gr.
  • Cheese 100 gr.
  • Meat 200 gr.
  • Carrot 50 gr.
  • Tomato paste 75 gr.

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook pasta for 10 mins
  2. Into a preheated pan add meat and cook it until brown
  3. Add chopped carrot and tomato paste, let it simmer
  4. Add pasta into the pan
  5. Enjoy!
