Alfredo

Description

This is an italian dish, made of pasta butter and cheese

Ingredients

  • Fettucine 200 gr.
  • Cheese 200 gr.
  • Butter 100 gr.
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta and boil for 10 mins
  3. Shred cheese and add butter into the bowl, heated by steam of the boiling pasta
  4. Add pasta and a bit pasta water into the bowl
  5. Add pepper and stir
  6. Enjoy!
