Lasagna

Description

This is an italian dish, made of layered pasta sheets, meat stew and cheese

Ingredients

  • Lasagna 300 gr.
  • Cheese 150 gr.
  • Meat 300 gr.
  • Carrot 150 gr.
  • Tomato paste 100 gr.

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add meat
  3. slice carrot and add into a pot
  4. wait 2 hours for it to simmer and add tomato paste
  5. boil lasagna
  6. layer meat paste on lasagna with shredded cheese and into the oven for 2 hours for
  7. Enjoy!
